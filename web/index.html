Argon - Experimental Social Network

Invite-only

Argon is an invite-only service meaning that if you want to join, you need to be invited. You click on a link that looks like https://argon.juaneth.xyz/invite/?1234567890 and you will be redirected to a page that prompts you to install the client and register an account. 1 Account per invite and 100 invites per account. Data collection

Argon has a policy of collecting as little data as possible. We only collect relevant and necessary data linked to your friends list and liked topics.

We all know a certain company that likes to harvest everyones data and implement ad trackers. We rely on donations made to us to monetise and will not sell your data or put ads.
